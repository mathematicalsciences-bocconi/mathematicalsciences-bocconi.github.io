Bocconi Student Association for Mathematical Sciences

We are Bocconi's first Math association, dedicated to fostering a vibrant community for students passionate about mathematics and more. Join us to engage deeply and learn together with peers, discovering and understanding new aspects of the subject.

Our association is based on networking, collaborations and research opportunities. We are developing a deep connection with the department of Decision Sciences, in order to create a bridge between students and professors. Regarding research, our long term project is to have 4 divisions:

  • Mathematics division
  • Statistics division
  • Applied Maths division (decision theory, game theory, mathematical economics)
  • Physics division
Each with its own board and activities.

Last but not least, we organize sessions to prepare for math competitions such as the ICMC, to represent Bocconi among other top universities.
